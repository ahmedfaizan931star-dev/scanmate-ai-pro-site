Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026 · App: ScanMate AI Pro · Developer: SynthByte Apps

ScanMate AI Pro is an Android document scanner app. This policy explains how the app is designed to handle documents, permissions, and optional features.

Core app behavior

The core scanning workflow is designed to work without creating an account, login, or cloud workspace. Documents, scans, OCR text, and exported files are intended to be processed and stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to share or export them.

Permissions

  • Camera: used to capture document scans.
  • Photos, media, or files: used to import images and save/export documents where Android requires permission.
  • Biometric or device security: may be used for local vault or protection features if enabled by the user.
  • Network: may be used for optional features such as opening policy/support links or optional AI features if enabled.

Documents and OCR

OCR and document tools are intended to run locally for the core app experience. The app does not require users to send documents to the developer for normal scanning, cropping, OCR, or export workflows.

Optional AI features

If optional AI features are available and the user chooses to enable them, selected content may be sent to the AI provider selected by the user or configured in the app. Users should not use optional AI features with sensitive documents unless they understand the provider’s policy and consent to that processing.

Data sharing

ScanMate AI Pro does not sell personal data. The app does not require a developer-hosted account system for the core document scanner.

Data retention and deletion

Scans and exported files remain on the device until the user deletes them, removes app data, or uninstalls the app. Users can also use Android system settings to clear local app data.

Security

The app aims to handle documents locally and responsibly. Users should still protect their device with a screen lock and avoid sharing sensitive files with untrusted apps or people.

Children

ScanMate AI Pro is a productivity tool and is not specifically directed at children.
