Data Safety Summary

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This page summarizes the app’s expected data behavior for users and store review. The Google Play Data Safety form should match the actual released app build.

AreaExpected behavior
AccountNo ScanMate account is required for core scanning features.
DocumentsDocuments are intended to be processed locally for core scanning, OCR, and export workflows.
CameraCamera access is used to scan documents.
Files/mediaFile access may be used to import images and save/export documents.
