Data Safety Summary

This page summarizes the current privacy position of ScanMate AI Pro for users. It should be kept consistent with the Google Play Data Safety form.

Core app

Core scanning, editing, OCR, and export workflows are designed to operate locally on the device without a required login or developer-operated cloud sync.

Permissions

Camera is used for scanning. File/media access is used for import and export. Biometric/security features may be used for local app protection workflows if enabled.

Optional AI

If optional AI tools are enabled by the user, selected content may be sent to the selected third-party AI provider. Users should review provider terms and privacy policies before enabling external processing.
