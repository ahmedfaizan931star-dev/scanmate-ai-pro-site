Delete Local Data

How to remove ScanMate AI Pro data from your device.

ScanMate AI Pro does not require an account for core features, so normal offline use does not create a developer-hosted account that must be deleted.

Delete documents inside the app

  1. Open ScanMate AI Pro.
  2. Find the document, page, QR history item, or export you want to remove.
  3. Use the delete/remove option where available.
  4. Check your device file manager for exported copies you may have saved separately.

Clear all app data using Android settings

  1. Open Android Settings.
  2. Go to Apps.
  3. Select ScanMate AI Pro.
  4. Open Storage.
  5. Tap Clear data or Clear storage.

This may delete local documents, settings, OCR history, saved API key, and other local app data.

Uninstall the app

Uninstalling the app may remove app-managed local data. Exported files saved outside the app folder may remain on your device until you delete them manually.
