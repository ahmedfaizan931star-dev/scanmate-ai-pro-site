Delete Local Data

ScanMate AI Pro

ScanMate AI Pro does not require a ScanMate account for core scanning features. Most app data is local to the device.

Delete documents inside the app

Open the app, review your documents, and delete scans or exports you no longer want to keep.

Clear app data from Android settings

You can also remove local app data from Android system settings:

  • Open Android Settings
  • Go to Apps
  • Select ScanMate AI Pro
  • Open Storage
  • Choose Clear data or Clear storage

Uninstall the app

Uninstalling the app may remove local app-managed data. Files exported to public folders may need to be deleted separately using a file manager.
